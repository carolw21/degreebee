My Projects

Pic-to-Word

This is a convenience iOS app coded in Swift that I am currently working on to publish in the App Store. It allows users to take or upload pictures of text and have them converted to digital text. The digital text can then be altered and sent by email, posted on Facebook, or uploaded to a document in Google Drive. It uses a handwriting recognition algorithm that is a work in progress.

This is a financial tracking/budgeting app coded in Swift at VMWare for the Girls Who Code Summer Program.
