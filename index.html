What is degreebee?

Degreebee helps students explore various majors, allowing them to find jobs related to a major, discover colleges that offer the major, and connect with those who have experience or expertise in that major.

Main Screen

This first screen shows general categories that users can click to discover majors under their category of interest.

List of Majors

This list includes all majors under the category clicked by the user.

Search Function

The search function allows users who already have an idea of a major to directly find their major of interest.

Major Information

This page offers detailed information about the major. There are short lists of the jobs related to the major, colleges that offer the major, and references who have experience with the major. As the user scrolls up, the toolbar collapses. Users can also share/save the major if they are interested in it.

List of Colleges

This is an example of the expanded list of colleges when the user clicks "Show All." The jobs and references lists follow a similar format.

Add Reference

References can be added dynamically to the database, and one only has to enter his/her name and email.
